Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 17, 2025, 09:58 PM EDT

Premier Pardons ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to safeguarding your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information under the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and other Canadian privacy laws. By using our website or services, you consent to these practices.

1. Information Collected

We collect: name, contact details, criminal record data, payment info (via third parties), and technical data (e.g., IP address, cookies) to support your application.

2. Use of Information

Your data is used for service delivery, communication, payment processing, legal compliance, and website improvement (via anonymized data). We do not sell it for marketing.

3. Data Security

We use encryption, secure servers, and ISO 27001-aligned measures to protect your data, though no system is fully immune to breaches.

4. Disclosure

Data may be shared with service providers, legal authorities, or successors in mergers, under confidentiality agreements or legal mandates.

5. Retention

Data is kept for the duration needed (e.g., 30 days post-case) or as required by law (e.g., 7 years for financial records), deletable on request if legally permissible.

6. Your Rights

Under PIPEDA, you can access, correct, or delete your data. Contact us at privacy@premierpardons.ca; we’ll respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

We use cookies for functionality. Disable them in your browser if preferred, though this may limit features.

8. International Transfers

Data may be transferred to the U.S. for waivers, governed by PIPEDA-compliant safeguards.

9. Children’s Privacy

Services are for those 19+. We delete minor data upon discovery.

10. Policy Changes

Updates will be posted with notice. Continued use signifies acceptance.
